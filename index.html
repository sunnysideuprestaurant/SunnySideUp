What is Sunny Side Up?

Sunny Side Up is a restaurant that takes breakfast to a whole new level! Along with our fresh, griddle-top cooked meals, we provide everyone access to our cool Pancake Bar. The Pancake Bar at Sunny Side Up is extremely special because it combines the excitement of a Hibachi Grill and uses those techniques to make cool Pancakes. Our Family Friendly Restaurant is meant for all people and we hope you enjoy it.

Concept

The beauty of this restaurant lays in its concept. This restaurant was created to take the concept of a traditional, family-friendly breakfast restaurant and add an interesting twist in it. To give people the spark they may need in the morning, we feel the best way to do that is to excite you in the morning. We're not talking about that excitement you get when you get your first cup of coffee, but we're talking about something that could really excite you. The best way to do that is to prepare food on a HIBACHI STYLE GRILL. Yes, that's right! We are integrating a Hibachi Style Grill with breakfast. In a unique style breakfast bar, we made a unique breakfast experience where your pancakes and eggs are cooked right in front of your face in a very theatrical fashion. It even has a special for the kids where our chefs will cook and form a pancake plate in special shapes! We feel like our restaurant can revolutionize the breakfast industry by making mornings feel less dull side down and much more Sunny Side Up!
